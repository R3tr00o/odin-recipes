Spaghetti

Description

Comforting baked spaghetti recipe with plenty of melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Salt

How to make Spaghetti

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the meat, then drain off the excess oil.
  3. Add sauce and salt.
  4. Toss the spaghetti in the Parmesan mixture
